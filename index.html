About Me

With over three decades of experience in the technology sector, I am a seasoned Senior Management Professional and serial entrepreneur. My career spans a diverse range of roles, from co-founding innovative startups to leading established organizations.

As a technology leader, I've spearheaded projects in cutting-edge fields such as IoT, Big Data analytics, and cloud-based solutions. My expertise lies in:

  • Driving profit centers and general management
  • Implementing global delivery models
  • Developing quality-driven processes
  • Leading business development initiatives
  • Establishing and managing operations

I've had the privilege of co-founding and leading companies like Medeva.io, where we're revolutionizing healthcare information management, and RYTLE, where we developed last-mile delivery solutions for the on-demand economy.

My experience also includes significant roles such as Managing Director & CEO at Everett India, where I expanded the company's reach across India, South East Asia, the Middle East, and Africa. As COO at LG CNS Global, I grew the Product Engineering and Oracle Apps practice for various international clients.

I'm passionate about leveraging technology to solve complex business challenges and am always eager to explore new opportunities for innovation and growth in the ever-evolving tech landscape.
